Why We Love Mint Chocolate Chip

Mint Chocolate Chip ice cream is a beloved classic. It combines the refreshing taste of mint with the rich, satisfying crunch of chocolate chips. Here’s why it’s a top pick for ice cream lovers!

Fun Facts About Mint Chocolate Chip

  • Mint Chocolate Chip was invented in the 1970s.
  • The ice cream typically contains a ratio of about 5% mint flavor and 15% chocolate.
  • It’s often green, but some varieties are white in color.
  • Mint is a natural digestive aid, so it’s perfect after a heavy meal!
